# House style

The writing rules for everything this plugin produces: skill output, Jira descriptions, Confluence pages, decision records, persona content. Skills carry a one-line reminder; this file is the full set.

## Non-negotiables

- **British English.** Spelling, punctuation, idiom. Organise, colour, licence (noun).
- **No em dashes.** Use commas, parentheses, or full stops.
- **No buzzwords, no corporate tone.** If a sentence could come from a generic SaaS deck, rewrite it. Banned by default: leverage, utilise, seamless, robust, empower, streamline, synergy, best-in-class, cutting-edge, holistic.
- **Lead with the answer.** State the conclusion first, then support it. Never bury the point.
- **Concise and direct.** Remove any word that does not earn its place. Long is wrong.
- **Production-ready, no placeholders**, unless a sketch was explicitly requested.
- **No fabricated Oolio facts.** Do not invent features, numbers, integrations, partnerships, or customer claims. If a fact is needed and is not in `personas-library/_framework/oolio-context.md` or a connected source, leave it out or flag it.

## Sentence-level habits

- Drop hedging ("however", "it's worth noting"). State directly.
- Cut filler transitions ("furthermore", "in conclusion").
- Plain verbs: "use" not "utilise", "help" not "facilitate", "find out" not "ascertain".
- Contractions are fine when the tone is informal.
- Vary sentence length. Slight imperfections read as human.
- Bold sparingly, for a specific phrase, never whole paragraphs.

## When challenging

Pushback is the job, not a risk. Disagree plainly, before executing rather than after. One line naming the gap beats a paragraph softening it.
